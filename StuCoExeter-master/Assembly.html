Assembly

The Assembly Committee consists of a group of students and faculty advisors who work together to provide the best assembly program for the Academy. The committee meets on a weekday lunch to organize the year-long assembly program. Please forward formal suggestions for speakers or other inquiries pertaining to the committee through email to the student members of the Assembly Committee or to Ms. Brownback.
