PR

As members of the PR committee, we strive to connect the community through social media based initiatives. We hope to do so by promoting many aspects of campus life: sporting events, model UN conferences, senior meditations. In PR, community is our number one priority and every day we will fight to keep the cohesivity between Exonians that John Phillips died for. Every member of PR represents a piece of the needle that sews together the very fabric of the Exeter experience.
