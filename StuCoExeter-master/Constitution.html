Constitution

The Constitution is the governing document of student council. All motions, actions, and members must follow its procedures and rules.

Laid out within it are the rules governing motions, amendments, elections, and committees. Every member of the Council should know this document back and forth. It holds the Executive Board and the whole council accountable.

When we do not follow the constitution, or neglect some of its clauses what frequently results is dysfunctional council.

The constitution, when necessary, can be amended with two-thirds of the council in support of that said amendment. Council members should pay special attention to the descriptions of positions, and the procedures for motions and amendments. The Executive Board also recommend all members to read about parliamentary procedure.
