Rec

Student Council's Recreation Committee is responsible for planning and running various school events around the Exeter campus. With the help of members in the committee as well as the faculty of Student Activities, we host events such as pep rallies, open-mic nights, and Spring Fling. We work with the rest of the Student Council to get approval on events and take any suggestions from the student body. Email jyun@exeter.edu (Jena Yun) and iedo@exeter.edu (Bella Edo) with any ideas or questions.
