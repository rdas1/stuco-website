DHall

The role of the Dining Hall Committee in the Student Council is to act as a liaison between the the respective staffs of our two dining halls, Wetherell and Elm, and the student body. Our goal is to enhance the dining experience for everyone in our Exeter community. We fulfill our goal by relaying the students’ feedback to the dining staff and vice versa. This year we wish to focus on how the student body can give back to the dining halls and learn more about the dining halls’ waste management and production.
