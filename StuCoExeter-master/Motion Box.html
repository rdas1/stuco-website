What is the Motion Box


A motion will be the first step to any proposal or legislation of the Student Council. If a student at Phillips Exeter Academy wishes the Council to act upon a certain issue, a motion is required. All motions must be submitted to the Vice-President in writing.


How to use the Motion Box


A motion can be formed and written by any member of the Phillips Exeter Academy student body; however, they must satisfy these rules for consideration:

  • Sponsored by a Class Representative or a Dorm Representative
  • Signed by two Members of Student Council: Executive officers, Class Representatives, Dorm Representatives, and Committee Chairs

A motion calling for a proposal to be presented and voted upon on the floor must have a written proposal attached. A specific header for proposals is available below.

Anything a student wishes the Student Body to assemble upon may be grounds for a motion for Student Council proceedings. All committee chairs wishing to use Student Council funds for duties/activities must submit a motion; there are no exceptions.

To submit a motion, please print out the template below, complete with legible handwriting—illegible handwriting will be sent back for re-completion—and submit it directly to the Student Council PO box (ask PO attendant). Motions that do not use this template will be rejected for procedural matters.

Motions can be

  • Accepted, presented to the Executive Board and the relevant Committee, if applicable, and brought to the Council Floor
  • Put on hold. Sponsor and co-sponsors will be summoned, by email, to a markup meeting with the Vice-President and Secretary to revise language and content of a proposal; following a markup meeting, the revised motion will be sent back to the sponsor and may be edited and resubmitted at any time. Sponsors of motions without an attached proposal will not be subjected to summons. Failure to show up to a markup meeting will result in automatic rejection of the motion and proposal, subject to appeal.
  • Rejected, at the discretion of the Vice-President.

A motion may request the use of Student Council funds for any purposes that affirm the E-book. If it is for a club or extracurricular student activities, complete the Request for Funds form located under the student council documents tab. If not, a direct motion to the Vice-President shall be entertained.

A motion can be any of these following, but not limited to:

  • A proposal for a school-wide policy change
  • A specific recognition of a certain student/team
  • An issue with a departmental curriculum
  • An official statement to the Administration

Please email the Vice-President if you have any questions, at hkim3@exeter.edu.
