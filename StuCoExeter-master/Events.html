Academy Life Day

September 26, 2016

All required appointments are canceled so that students can take part in residential activities. Each dorm organizes its own outing to facilitate residential bonding. These activities range from a day at the beach to dodgeball to apple-picking. It is a day for students to take a break from the monotony of classes and to become closer to their dormmates.

Classes Begin

September 9, 2016

After all students check back in to their dorms on the night of the eight they will recieve their class schedules. The next day, the first all-student assembly will be held in the morning and regular classes will begin. The first day back is usually a for students to familiarize themselves with each class and to recieve their first homework assignments.

Back to School

September 7, 2016 - September 8, 2016

New students will arive on campus on the 7th for orientation. They will be greated by their proctors who will assist them with the move-in process. The following day, returning students will arrive on to camus. Check-in is held each day from 9AM to 2PM. students are expected to be unpacked and ready for classes on the 9th of September.
