Budget

We've got funds, and you need them! So fill out an application and come to us. We'll set up a short meeting to talk about your club and idea! We support events that benefit the educational mission of the school, whether direct or indirect. Here are the steps of funding:

  • Have a compelling student driven initiative that needs funding.
  • Fill out the funding application with details of the request.
  • Come up with a joke related to your initiative.
  • Email the above information to mmcshea@exeter.edu and imatsumoto@exeter.edu.
  • Schedule a meeting with Matt and Issay.
  • Get money.
  • Make Exeter awesome.

So click on the link below and get funding for your club today.
