Mission and Purpose

The Mission of this council is to stand as a representative form of student government and a channel of communication between students and any other school constituency. Our purpose is to give a voice to the student body and to provide a platform through which students and faculty can share their ideas. This year, the Executive board plans to adress the many problems surrounding sexual assault on the Exter campus and, also, to improve the efficiency of the council through constitutional changes.
