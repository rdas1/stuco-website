About the Department Feedback Center

The feedback center is platform for the student body to communicate with and provide feedback to different constituencies on campus. The forms below are powered by Formsite and designed by the departments listed. Your feedback will be sent anonymously to the specified department. For any further questions about this process please contact Jackson Parell, Secretary, at jparell@exeter.edu.
