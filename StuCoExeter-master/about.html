Phillips Exeter Academy Student Council

The Phillips Exeter Academy Student Council is a representative form of student government and a channel of communication between students and any other school constituency.

The council consists of the Executive board (President, Vice-President, and Secretary), committee heads, class representatives, dormitory/day student representatives, and Select members.

The greater entity of PEAâ€™s Student Council is subdivided into 9 committees. Each committee handles different responsibilities of the council.

Meeting are hosted on Tuesday Evenings from 8-9 in the Forum to discuss committee reports, motions brought to the floor, or other topics relating to the duties of student government.

The student council is meant to be the voice of the students and to represent their thoughts, concerns, and ideas in all political issues.
